Athens

Single Player, Greek Shooter Whitebox

This was a university assignment that I completed as part of my 'Level Design' module during year 2. The assignment had us create a white box level for a Capture Point game that would be played through the 'Lyra' addon for Unreal Engine 5.

The Map

I chose to base my design off the city of Athens in Greece with heavy inspiration taken from the ancient city structures such as the Parthenon. Each of the large white buildings house a capture point that both teams battle to control in order to gain points, weapons are scattered around the map with a powerful grenade launcher being found at the top of the central building.

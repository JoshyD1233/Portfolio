Single Player, Escape Simulator Escape Room

This is a level that I created within the game Escape Simulator during my third year at university as part of my 'Personal Portfolio' module. The aim of said module was to select an area of the games industry to develop your skills in and produce a piece of work for.

The level itself is Victorian themed with lots of dark red and brown colours present within the level itself lending itself to colours found within the Victorian era.

Room 1 - Games Room

The Games Room is where the player starts the escape room. It has been designed to fit the victorian theming wiht the inclusion of a large desk, styled sofas, a fireplace and various victorian paintings.

These are the main puzzles within the Games Room, they consist of:

  • Solitaire - four missing cards are hidden around the room. They must be found and placed correctly onto the game of Solitaire.
  • Aztec Wall Art - There are four missing buttons around the room. By pressing one its corresponding colour starts spinning on the wall art. the puzzle is completed when all four coloured elements are spinning.
  • Grandfather Clock - The Grandfather Clock needs to be reset by finding hints and numbers hidden around the room using the tags on the clock's weights.
  • Bookshelf - The books missing from the shelf have to be found around the room and placed in the correct spots by using hints also found around the room.

    • Room 2 - Kitchen

    The Kitchen is the second room, in this room the goal is to set the table with missing food, drinks and cutlery. The player must use the kitchen itself to assemble the required items.

    Key puzzles within the Kitchen include:

  • Cocktail mixing - Finding ingredients and recipies in order to create soecific drinks for the table
  • Cooking Food - Using ingredients, the mixing bowl and the oven dishes are made for the table
  • Ordering Takeaway - By using the phone and finding the correct phone numbers, the player needs to order the correct food for the fussy dinner guests
  • Finding Correct Recipies - Within the list of cookbooks the correct recipe has to be found in order to make the correct food

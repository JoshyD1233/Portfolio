Level Designer

Polaris-13

Single Player & Online Multiplayer, Survival Space Game

This project saw me take on the primary role of Level Designer within my team 'Absolute Entertainment'. As the game's spaceship is the central focus of the game, a large portion of my time was spent planning, designing and laying out the ship's internals and its subsequent 10 rooms. Alongside this I planned out and designed the tutorial level. Other responsibilities I held within this project include:

  • Creation of the game's Points Of Interest - 5 total
  • Designing and Implementing the Crafting System into Unity
  • Helped set up Player Animation through Unity's Animation Tree
  • Assisted in Texturing 3D models - Primarily for craftable items
  • Modelled, Textured and set up the lighting in both the Ship and the Points Of Interest

    Tutorial Level

    The Tutorial Level is designed to highlight the game's most important mechanics to the player. These include:

  • Player Movement - Both with and without gravity
  • Interactable Objects - Chests containing items and how to add them to their inventory
  • Collecting/Crafting with resources - Turning items into something new
  • Equipable Items - Using weapons/tools

    • The tutorial itself takes roughly 5 - 10 minutes to complete based on the players skill level. Giving the player a breif showcase of the most critical mechanics within the game allows them to take in the environments designed in the main game without feeling overwhelmed.

    Points Of Interest

    The Points of Interest are key locations that can be found by players often through portals that spawn around the ship amongst the debris. They offer a mix of different encounter types with some including skill based challenges such as parkour and others being more exploration driven these points of interest consist of:

  • Orbit Puzzle - A puzzle that blends both gravity and anti-gravity movement to navigate orbiting debris with loot being rewarded in the center.
  • Space Ship Ruins - An exploration driven encounter that has hidden chests for players to discover as well as enemies to evade
  • Meteor Building - A building in disaray that gives the player more information on the game's background story as well as items and loot
  • Parkour Islands - A challenging anti-gravity parkour experience that requires player awareness to avoid floating meteors and rewards the players with high tier loot
  • Goober Asteroid - An asteroid that can spawn alongside the ship covered in enemies and does not give the player any form of positive reward

    • While creating the points of interest I wanted to create a diverse set of experiences that kept each encounter unique and prevented the points of interest from feeling similar to each other.

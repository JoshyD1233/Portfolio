Rampage Of The Hydra

Single Player, Narrative Driven Medieval Experience

This was a piece of work done for my 'Narrative Design' module, the aim was to create a playable experience that tells a Narrative through a combination of factors such as character dialogue and level design. The experience was created using the 'Survival RPG Engine' created by Mongo Games in Unreal 5.

The Narrative

The Narrative itself sees the player play as a hero who has stumbled across a village that has just been attacked by a Hydra (Shown through broken/pushed over buildings) they then need to gear up and make their way to the Hydra cave killing skeletons along the way. Once they reach the cave they need to force the cave to collapse. Depending on how they achieve this decides which ending they get.

The Environment

Above is a collection of screenshots from the level itself which is split up into three distinct sections, these are:

  • The Village - Where the majority of the dialogue of the game is, the player spends the most time here.
  • The Path - Most of the small enemy encounters are found in this section. Designed to act as a connecting zone between the two other zones.
  • The Hydra Cave - The final section of the game. Featuring a large cave with the final boss hiding within. The player can choose their ending in this area.

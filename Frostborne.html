Frostborne

Single Player, Viking Level Whitebox

This is a white box that I created as part of the second assignment for my Level Design module at university. This was a two person module with each team member taking on a level each for a theme and narrative or their choosing. My level was the second level of our 'Viking adventure' narrative.

The Story

In our narrative you play as a lone Viking warrior who's village was attacked in the first level once you get to my level the narrative becomes one of revenge as said Viking lands on the shores of the village belonging to the Vikings that raided his village with one single aim, to avenge his lost people.

You work your way through the forest, sneaking past and/or slaying enemies all along the way, upon moving through the forest and through the remainder of the level you reach the end where you make a decision on whether to spare the remaining enemy Vikings or slay them. Each choice gives a different ending.
